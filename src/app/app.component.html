We met through school, well kinda...

Tyler remembers Sam from his first packaging science class at Clemson. Tyler and Sam both declared their major as Graphic Communications and shared only a few classes together during their time at Clemson. Tyler would try and make small talk with Sam, but Sam was off in her own world during most of the time and did not realize the "small talk" was Tyler's attempt to eventually ask her out. Tyler never did ask Sam out during those 3 years at Clemson, he felt the occasional small talk was not enough to grab Sam's attention. It wasn't until their last day of final exams where these two hit it off. After the last exam of their Clemson Undergraduate, a small group went to get breakfast on campus to celebrate together. At breakfast, everyone agreed to go out for one last hoorah before summer began. That night, Sam kissed Tyler and that was where the sparkle began! Unfortunately, Tyler was leaving the day after to visit his family in Germany for the summer, as well as to visit the Esko HQ in Ghent, Belgium. When Tyler came back from Europe, he immediately started working with Esko and was now living in Dayton, OH. Throughout the summer, there would be a text here and there, as well as a continuous Facebook message thread, but nothing serious…yet. That was until Tyler invited Sam for a weekend getaway in Nashville and Sam initially said "yes". Unfortunately, the week of the planned trip, Sam was sick with strep throat and had to cancel last minute. Luckily, Tyler was able to get his hotel reservation cancelled and was not charged for the weekend ;). Tyler's second attempt at a date with Sam was asking her to be his date a mutual friend's wedding that October (a few months later). Sam considered the offer but told Tyler she was not interested in driving 16 hours from South Carolina to Indiana for a weekend. Tyler's third (and possibly final) attempt was a wedding invite in November in Sam's own back yard, Greenville, South Carolina. Sam finally said "yes". Sam remembers getting ready for this wedding and was very nervous arriving to the Westin in downtown Greenville. Little did she know; Tyler's entire family was also attending the wedding! Regardless, this night sealed the deal with lots of laughter, chatting, drinking and a good-night kiss. This is truly where it all began… After the first wedding date, there were several subsequent dates where the two continued to grow closer, become best friends, and eventually fall in love. From weekend getaways at the Biltmore, trips to Orlando, Sam visiting Ohio, Tyler visiting South Carolina, Tyler teaching Sam to snowboard, trips to Europe, scuba in Aruba, exploring Italy, and many other exciting adventures, there was never a dull moment and they became inseparable. Fast forward to 2018 and the almost-college sweethearts are ready to say, "I do", in Tyler's families home-state of Nebraska. We appreciate you reading how we met and how we fell in love. We can't wait to share this love and celebrate our special day with you! We look forward to seeing each and every one of you! Much love, Sammy and Ty
